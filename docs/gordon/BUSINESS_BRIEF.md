A **system** (not a tool) that:

2. **Organizes** it into searchable, auditable form
3. **Extracts** patterns and insights automatically
4. **Proves** competency objectively (not resume claims)

**Result:**
- Onboarding in weeks instead of months
- Decision audit trail (why did we choose this?)
- Better hiring (competency markers work across industries)
- Knowledge preserved when people leave
